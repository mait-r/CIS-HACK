How to Protect?

I the contemporary scenario taking precautions is the key of saving us from the pandemic.

We the people must not forget to

  • Wear mask
  • Maintain Social distancing

But many a times it has been observed that people don't follow the covid norms and government agencies have to work alot in making people follow the norms.

Thus FlashNode presents Face Mask and Distancing Alert Software namely: Covid Alert.

Covid Alert

It is a free resource developed by First Year students of Maharaja Agrasen Institute of Technology to facilitate government agencies to prevent the spread of novel Coronavirus. Our software detects the people who are not following social distancing and who are not wearing a mask. Thus, making the task of government agencies a lot easier. It helps to maintain law and order. We also provide the protocols issued by government agencies for the ease of the user so that they always follow the Covid- 19 norms. Information about number of Covid cases is updated daily here. Moreover, we also provide a piece of advice to the user about how to make a safer choice. We also provide latest news updates in video format. You can also get doctor’s advice and a great general knowledge about covid-19.
